# BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES
- Tone: Calm, serious, investigative.
- Style: Minimal adjectives, no hype, no "You won't believe".
- Structure: Cause -> Effect. Physical actions.
- Language: Simple spoken English. 80% sentences <= 12 words.
- Forbidden: "amidst", "unease began to stir", "picture of serenity", "claims emerged", "was left to question", "with unimaginable fury", "little did he know".
- Focus: Human POV. What did they see/hear/feel?
